How it works

First, Moviefy will analyze your recent tweets or your custom text using the advanced techniques of text processing and data mining.

Then, after completing the analysis, Moviefy's super powerful Machine Learning algorithm, will use your recent tweets/text to detect your current emotional and feeling status.

Finally, with the help of the acquired data, Moviefy will try to recommend you some movies based on the result of your emotional status.

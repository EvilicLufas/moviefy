Moviefy


When Machine Learning can tell you what movies to watch!


Moviefy, a machine learning based movie recommendation system which facilitates the process of choosing certain movie to watch and thinking about its rating, review ... etc.

Any user now can simply signin through their twitter account and then Moviefy will propose for them exciting movies to watch according to their feelings.

Also, without having an account anyone can TRY by simply writing their own text and click on analyze button and get awesome recommendation from Moviefy.

Finally, Moviefy will continue to develop with the help of its users. We will continue to enhance the algorithm based on the results it gains from running on many users around the globe.
